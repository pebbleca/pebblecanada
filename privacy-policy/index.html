Privacy Policy

How Pebble Canada collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Effective date: March 2026  |  Last reviewed: March 2026

Pebble Canada (Pebble CA Inc.) is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who interact with our website and services. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and your rights under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the personal information you voluntarily provide to us. The primary means of collection is the contact form on our website, which asks for:

  • › Your name
  • › Your email address
  • › A subject line and message

We do not collect payment information, government identification, or any sensitive personal information through this website. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not use your personal information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent, and we do not sell, rent, or trade your information to any third party.

3. Third-Party Form Processing

Our contact form is processed by Formspree, a third-party form submission service. When you submit the form, your message is transmitted through Formspree's infrastructure before being delivered to us. Formspree's privacy policy governs how they handle data in transit. We encourage you to review it at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

4. Data Retention

Enquiries received via the contact form are retained only as long as necessary to respond to and resolve the matter raised. We do not maintain a marketing database or subscriber list from website submissions.

5. Your Rights Under PIPEDA

Under PIPEDA, you have the right to:

  • › Know what personal information we hold about you
  • › Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • › Withdraw consent for the use of your personal information (where applicable)
  • › File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada if you believe your privacy rights have been violated

6. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns, please contact us at:

hello@pebblecanada.ca

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
