About Pebble Canada

A Canada-focused organization advancing capability across technology, logistics, defence, and workforce.

Pebble Canada is a Canada founded, globally active solutions organization supporting complex programs across technology, logistics and supply chains, defence capability, and workforce delivery for energy and critical minerals.

Established in 2025, Pebble Canada was formed to address a recurring gap seen across both public and private sector initiatives: strong strategy and investment intent, but insufficient execution capacity in high consequence operating environments. From day one, the organization was designed around delivery discipline, operational accountability, and the ability to scale capability rapidly across borders and sectors.

Today, Pebble Canada operates with a global footprint, supported by a network of hundreds of consultants, specialists, and delivery professionals spanning North America, Europe, Asia, and emerging markets. This distributed model allows us to deploy the right expertise at the right time, while maintaining centralized governance, quality control, and client accountability.

We work with government organizations, prime contractors, infrastructure developers, energy operators, manufacturers, and technology providers who operate under real constraints: regulatory oversight, security expectations, labour shortages, supply chain volatility, and tight delivery timelines. Our role is to convert complexity into structured, auditable, and repeatable execution.


Our Four Pillars

Technology, Automation & AI

Pebble Canada delivers applied technology solutions focused on operational enablement rather than experimentation. Our work spans automation, data platforms, analytics, and AI driven decision support, implemented with security, governance, and adoption in mind.

We help organizations modernize systems, improve visibility, and reduce manual overhead while ensuring technology aligns with regulatory, privacy, and operational realities.

Logistics & Supply Chain

We design and operate resilient supply chains for trade exposed, infrastructure heavy, and mission critical environments. Our logistics capability supports procurement, international trade corridors, supplier diversification, and end to end delivery coordination.

Pebble Canada emphasizes traceability, continuity planning, and execution visibility to support both operational performance and stakeholder confidence.

Defence & Advanced Systems

Pebble Canada supports defence readiness through structured procurement support, sustainment coordination, and advanced capability integration. Engagements range from essential equipment and services through emerging systems and platforms.

Our approach reflects the realities of defence environments: interoperability requirements, security constraints, lifecycle planning, and compliance driven delivery.

Workforce & Capability Solutions

We deliver workforce and capability solutions for energy, oil, and critical minerals across exploration, construction, operations, and downstream activities. This includes skilled labour deployment, technical specialists, and project based workforce mobilization.

Pebble Canada integrates workforce planning with safety standards, labour compliance, and operational continuity to support long term capability, not just short term staffing.

National Alignment

Aligned with Canada's Major Projects Office

Pebble Canada is positioned to support projects advanced under Canada's Major Projects Office (MPO) and the Building Canada Act — the federal framework for identifying and advancing nation-building projects of national interest. As Canada accelerates investment in critical minerals, energy infrastructure, trade connectivity, and advanced industrial capacity, our four delivery pillars are structured to provide the execution capability these programmes demand.

The MPO's mandate is to streamline federal approvals and advance transformative projects — including small modular reactors, critical minerals extraction and processing, and major trade and energy infrastructure — representing over $60 billion in planned Canadian investment. These projects require delivery partners with demonstrated capability across supply chain coordination, workforce mobilization, regulatory-compliant technology, and procurement execution at programme scale.

Workforce Mobilization for Nation-Building Projects

Pebble Canada's domestic and offshore workforce delivery capability supports the large-scale labour requirements of MPO-listed projects, including nuclear, mining, and energy infrastructure programmes with significant skilled trades demand in remote and regulated environments.

Supply Chain Coordination for Major Infrastructure

Our international logistics capability supports the materials sourcing, multimodal transport, and supplier diversification requirements of major capital projects — including those procuring equipment and components across Indo-Pacific and European supply corridors.

Technology & Regulatory Documentation

Major project proponents require data platforms, compliance documentation systems, and AI-driven decision support to manage the regulatory, reporting, and audit obligations that accompany federal approval processes under the Building Canada Act.

Defence-Adjacent Procurement Readiness

Some projects of national interest intersect with defence readiness and national security infrastructure. Pebble Canada's procurement capability and familiarity with Government of Canada solicitation frameworks is directly applicable to these programme requirements.
