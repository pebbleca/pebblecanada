Government Alignment

Major Projects of National Interest

The Government of Canada's Major Projects Office (MPO) advances transformative nation-building initiatives — including small modular reactors, critical minerals extraction, and strategic trade infrastructure — representing over $60 billion in planned national investment under the Building Canada Act. These projects span the full scope of Pebble Canada's four service pillars.

Our capabilities are structured to serve each phase of the MPO's seven-step Projects of National Interest designation process — from initial scoping and regulatory engagement through construction mobilization and operational ramp-up:

Technology & Data Platforms

Programme data management, reporting dashboards, and AI-enabled permitting documentation for complex multi-stakeholder projects — supporting the Application Review and Monitoring phases.

Workforce Mobilization

Skilled labour deployment for large-scale capital projects including nuclear, mining, and infrastructure — covering trades, technical specialists, and safety personnel for the Construction & Operations phase.

Logistics & Supply Chain

Equipment and materials sourcing for remote and industrial project sites — including critical minerals logistics supporting the Nouveau Monde Graphite Matawinie Mine and comparable facilities.

Defence & Security Procurement

Dual-use infrastructure, security systems procurement, and PSPC-compliant solicitation support for projects with a national security dimension — including the Darlington New Nuclear Project.
