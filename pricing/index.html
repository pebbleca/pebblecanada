Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Click "Manage Account" above to open your billing dashboard. From there you can cancel in one click. There are no cancellation fees, no phone calls, and no hoops to jump through. Your service continues until the end of your current billing period.

Is there a contract or minimum commitment?

No. Every plan is month-to-month. You can cancel at the end of any billing cycle and owe nothing further. We earn your business every month.

What happens after I subscribe?

Within 24 hours we'll reach out to schedule a short onboarding call. We'll confirm your channels, voice preferences, and priority topics. You'll see a sample week of posts for approval before anything goes live.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade from your account dashboard at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing date.

Do I need to give you access to my social accounts?

Yes, we'll need publishing access to the channels included in your plan. We use secure platform-native permissions and never ask for your personal login credentials.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards. Payments are securely processed through Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

All prices are in USD. Plans renew monthly. You can cancel or change your plan at any time from your account dashboard. Need a custom plan? Get in touch.
