Federal Digital Delivery

Delivering Technology Within Canada's Digital Government Framework

Our team understands the approval pathways, security categorization requirements, and interoperability standards that govern technology deployment in federal environments. Whether supporting a department's AI adoption roadmap, building analytics infrastructure on approved cloud platforms, or delivering digital services against WCAG and GC design standards, we operate within the rules of the road that federal clients require.

Regulated AI & Algorithmic Accountability

The Government of Canada's Directive on Automated Decision-Making requires AIA completion, bias review, and explainability documentation for AI systems that affect individuals. Pebble Canada builds AIA-ready AI systems, incorporating audit trails, human-in-the-loop checkpoints, and decision-log infrastructure from day one of delivery.

GC Cloud & Data Architecture

Federal data and analytics workloads must operate within approved cloud frameworks (Protected B, CCCS Medium) with appropriate data residency and access controls. Pebble Canada architects data platforms that meet these constraints, enabling departments to unlock AI and analytics capability without compromising their security posture or GC interoperability requirements.
