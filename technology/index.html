Projects of National Interest

Technology & Data Capability for Major Project Environments

Projects advanced through Canada's Major Projects Office (MPO) operate under the Building Canada Act's streamlined approval framework — which consolidates federal conditions into a single conditions document and targets a two-year approval timeline. Meeting these timelines requires proponents and delivery partners to maintain rigorous documentation, compliance tracking, and regulatory reporting infrastructure from day one.

Pebble Canada's enterprise data platform, AI-enabled reporting, and governance-oriented delivery model are directly applicable to the technology requirements of major project environments. We support the data architecture, decision support systems, and compliance documentation workflows that enable proponents and their delivery teams to manage multi-stakeholder approval processes, audit obligations, and operational reporting at programme scale.

Regulatory Documentation & Compliance Tracking

Major projects under the Building Canada Act are subject to consolidated conditions documents and multi-department oversight. AI-assisted document processing, compliance tracking dashboards, and audit-ready reporting systems reduce the administrative burden on proponents and help ensure conditions are being met on schedule.

Programme Data Platforms

Enterprise data platforms that integrate project management, supply chain visibility, workforce reporting, and financial oversight provide the cross-programme insight that major capital projects require. Pebble Canada builds centralized data environments that give programme leadership the visibility to identify risks and act decisively across complex, multi-party delivery structures.
