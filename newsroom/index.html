Pebble Canada Expands Logistics Capability Across Indo-Pacific Trade Corridors

March 2026

Pebble Canada has formalized strategic logistics partnerships supporting eastbound and westbound trade routes for Canadian operators. These partnerships enable clients to diversify trade flows across Asia-Pacific and European markets while maintaining compliance with customs, tariff, and documentation requirements. The expanded logistics capability supports critical minerals operators, energy companies, and technology organizations scaling international operations.

Pebble Canada Activates Critical Minerals Workforce Delivery Program

February 2026

Pebble Canada has launched a structured offshore workforce pipeline supporting Canadian critical minerals and energy projects. The program brings together pre-vetted international candidate pools across skilled trades, safety certifications, and deployment-ready personnel. With 13,000+ candidates in active database across multiple geographies and trade categories, the program enables operators to close workforce gaps without expanding permanent headcount. See our Critical Minerals Workforce analysis for strategic context.

Pebble Canada Registered on CanadaBuys / SAP Ariba for Government Procurement

January 2026

Pebble Canada is now registered and active on CanadaBuys (SAP Ariba), enabling participation in Government of Canada procurement solicitations. We are committed to understanding PSPC policy, Treasury Board governance, and the compliance requirements that govern public sector engagements. Our vendor registration marks a strategic milestone in our capacity to serve federal government departments and agencies.

Defence Practice Achieves Procurement Readiness Under DND Delivery Framework

January 2026

Pebble Canada's defence practice has achieved full procurement readiness to compete in Department of National Defence and Public Services and Procurement Canada (PSPC) solicitations. This includes ITT (Invitation to Tender), RFP (Request for Proposal), and RFQ (Request for Quotation) processes. We maintain facility security clearances, understand controlled goods program requirements, and have built compliance infrastructure supporting large-scale defence procurement.
